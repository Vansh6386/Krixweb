Our Story

A Passion for Authentic Flavors

Founded in 2018 by Chef Arjun Mehta, The Olive Kitchen was born from a simple belief: great food brings people together. Every dish on our menu is crafted using locally sourced ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

From our wood-fired oven to our herb garden, every detail is designed to deliver an unforgettable dining experience. We don't just serve food — we create memories.
